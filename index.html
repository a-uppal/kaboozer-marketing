Your family's schedule lives in one app, the grocery list in another, chores on a whiteboard nobody reads, and "can you grab this while you're out?" gets buried in the group chat. It shouldn't take this much effort to keep your own family on the same page.

Everything in one place

Five things your family needs daily

📅

Shared Calendar

Everyone's schedule, color-coded by family member. Recurring events, agenda view, and no more "I didn't know you had practice."

🛒

Shopping Lists

Add items from anywhere. Check them off in real time. The whole family sees the list update live while someone is already at the store.

✅

Chores & Points

Assign chores, track who actually did them, and award points. Weekly leaderboards turn "take out the trash" into a little friendly competition.

🍽️

Dinner Plans

See tonight's plan, mark who's eating, choose cook or takeout, and stop the 5pm "what's for dinner?" scramble before it starts.

📍

Store Check-Ins

Tap check in when you're at the store, and your family gets a notification with a link to the right shopping list. Simple, accurate, and actually useful.

Built by a family, for families

Kaboozer started with a familiar moment: one person was already at the store, someone texted "can you grab milk?", someone else remembered three more things, and the actual list was split across memory, messages, and another app. We wanted one place for schedules, shopping, chores, dinner, and the everyday coordination that usually slips through the cracks.
